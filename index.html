About me

Hello, my name is Katerina Ailamaki and i am a graphic designer from Greece. My design journey began at the University of West Attica, where I cultivated a deep love for graphic design. While exploring web design during my studies, I crafted an e-shop using Adobe XD, sparking a passion I further pursued post-graduation. Dedicated to enhancing my skills, I delved into coding with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and mastered the intricacies of WordPress.Although I haven't yet stepped into the professional world, my dedication and enthusiasm are unwavering. With a blend of creativity and attention to detail, I'm eager to contribute my skills to the dynamic world of graphic and web design.
